Product Overview

The Central Operations and Communications Hub is a centralized platform for managing maritime operational information and communications. It integrates data from multiple field teams, provides a unified operational overview, and supports coordination and informed decision making across the organization.

Key Capabilities

Maritime Operations Ecosystem Operational View

Provides a unified view of the location, status, and relevant information of participating assets and contacts within a defined maritime operational area. This capability delivers a comprehensive operational overview, enabling users to maintain clear visibility of ongoing activities.

Harbor Operations Reporting Hub

A shore based platform that facilitates operational reporting from field teams to the central operations center. It provides a structured approach to recording activities, events, observations, and follow up actions, ensuring information is captured in a consistent, reliable, and well organized format.
