End-to-end AI-powered platform for large-scale data collection, analysis, and strategic decision-making

Platform Overview

This end-to-end platform leverages advanced AI to collect and process large-scale data from diverse sources. It delivers comprehensive insights that combine demographic, behavioral, and social perspectives, supporting effective audience mapping and strategic decision-making.

Equipped with cutting-edge biometric and profiling capabilities, the system enables accurate identification and broad demographic analysis at scale. This integration ensures high reliability and provides organizations with a strong advantage, planning, and operational execution.

Core Modules

  • ▪ Federated Search and Face Matching with AI-powered social media identification
  • ▪ AI-Assisted Account Selection using multi-modal verification
  • ▪ Behavioural Pattern Analysis for risk assessment and profiling
  • ▪ Media Crawling for comprehensive background intelligence
  • ▪ Watchlist from Public Sources for enhanced verification

Application Use Cases

National Security Operations

Integrate identity, residence, and family data to track high-value targets, validate identities, and uncover hidden networks for counter terrorism and law enforcement operations.

Border & Immigration Control

Combine biometric recognition with demographic profiling for accurate identification of travelers, prevention of identity fraud, and monitoring of population movements across borders.

Smart City & Public Safety

Geo-demographic mapping and crowd density analysis help manage urban development, optimize resource allocation, and enhance public safety measures in crowded or high-risk areas.

Cyber & Social Media Monitoring

Support detection of disinformation campaigns, identification of key influencers, and real-time monitoring of online activities that may pose security or reputational threats.

Policy & Strategic Decision-Making

Provide governments, NGOs, and institutions with accurate data-driven insights to design targeted interventions, social programs, and long-term development strategies.
