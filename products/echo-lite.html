ECHO-Lite

Essential Short-Range Cellular Disruption Tool

Product Overview

ECHO-Lite is a mobile signal blocking solution designed to support close range cellular disruption in controlled environments. The system enables localized signal suppression through a compact and straightforward operational design, supporting rapid deployment and direct user control.

Engineered for short-range applications, ECHO-Lite emphasizes focused operation, calibrated output power, and ease of use, making it suitable for environments requiring immediate and contained communication isolation.

Primary Features

  • ▪
    8-Channel Micro Disruptor: Utilizes eight RF channels to support flexible and lightweight signal disruption within a confined operational area.
  • ▪
    Calibrated Output Power: Operates at controlled transmission power levels to support close-range cellular signal suppression.
  • ▪
    Preset Frequency Band: Configured to operate on a predefined LTE sub-band to ensure consistent and stable operation.
  • ▪
    Integrated Antenna Configuration: Employs a standard stub antenna design optimized for compact form factor and ease of deployment.
  • ▪
    User-Specific Campaign Configuration: Supports creation and execution of user-defined operational campaigns tailored to specific deployment requirements.
