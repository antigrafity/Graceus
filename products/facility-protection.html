Facility Protection

RF monitoring, electro-optical observation, and GNSS environment systems for airspace and site awareness

Overview

Facility Protection is a suite of monitoring and observation systems designed to improve visibility and situational awareness around designated facilities. Spanning vehicle mounted, handheld, and fixed installations, the suite combines RF monitoring, electro-optical and thermal observation, and GNSS environment management to help organizations understand local spectrum and airspace conditions and coordinate activities more effectively.
