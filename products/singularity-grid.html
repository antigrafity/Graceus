SINGULARITY GRID

Integrated Digital Forensics Platform for Complete Investigation Lifecycle

Platform Overview

SINGULARITY GRID is an integrated digital forensics platform that supports investigations from data acquisition to final reporting, covering computer, mobile, database, and video forensics, including decryption and data recovery. All evidence is securely stored to ensure integrity, while investigation results are consolidated into a centralized analytics dashboard that enables data correlation, case monitoring, and streamlined reporting.

Each forensic module is supported by a secure digital storage facility (Evidentiary Tower) to maintain chain of custody and guarantee the authenticity of evidence throughout the investigation lifecycle.

Forensic Modules

Computer Forensics

Analyzes system logs to uncover suspicious activity, repairs corrupted files for evidentiary use, and secures all digital evidence in an encrypted Evidentiary Tower to ensure data integrity.

Decryption Facility

Unlocks encrypted data using a forensically sound Decryptor System. All decrypted results are securely logged and stored in the Evidentiary Tower to guarantee authenticity.

Data Restoration

Recovers data from damaged or corrupted storage media, utilizing a Data Salvage Solution for HDDs/SSDs and a portable Flash Recovery Device for USB drives and memory cards.

Digital Video Investigation

Extracts and recovers footage from CCTV/DVR systems. This module enhances video quality, verifies authenticity to detect tampering, and securely stores the results in the Evidentiary Tower.

Mobile Forensics

Performs comprehensive forensic analysis of mobile devices. Capabilities include full data acquisition, deleted file recovery, and bypassing locked screens to ensure complete data extraction.

Database Forensics

Analyzes, recovers, and extracts critical data directly from system or application databases. All findings are systematically organized for comprehensive investigation support.

Big Data Analysis Platform

Integrates all forensic modules for powerful, cross-case analysis. It correlates data from various reports and includes a complete case management system, monitoring, managing the chain of custody, and generating automated reports.

Evidentiary Tower

The Evidentiary Tower serves as a secure digital storage facility that ensures the integrity of all evidence collected across forensic modules. All digital evidence is encrypted, logged, and systematically organized to guarantee authenticity and maintain chain-of-custody requirements throughout the investigation lifecycle.
